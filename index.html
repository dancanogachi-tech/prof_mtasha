Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our services, process, and what to expect.

How do I book a consultation?

You can book a consultation by calling us directly, sending a WhatsApp message, or filling out the contact form on our website. We'll respond within 24 hours to schedule your confidential session.

What should I expect during my first visit?

Your first visit includes a private consultation where Professor Mtasha will listen to your concerns, assess your situation, and provide an initial diagnosis. You'll receive a clear understanding of the recommended treatment plan, timeline, and expected outcomes.

Are your remedies safe and natural?

Absolutely! We use only 100% natural herbs and ingredients, sourced ethically from trusted suppliers. All remedies are prepared using traditional methods passed down through generations. We never use harmful chemicals or synthetic substances.

How long does it take to see results?

Results vary depending on the individual and the nature of the concern. Some clients experience immediate relief, while others see gradual improvement over weeks. We provide ongoing support and make adjustments as needed to ensure the best possible outcome.

Is my information kept confidential?

Yes, we take your privacy very seriously. All consultations, personal information, and treatment details are kept strictly confidential. We never share your information with third parties.

Do you offer online consultations?

Yes, we offer remote consultations via phone, WhatsApp, or video call for clients who cannot visit us in person. Many of our services can be performed remotely with the same effectiveness as in-person sessions.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, M-Pesa, and bank transfers. Payment details are provided during the booking process. We believe in transparent pricing with no hidden fees.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

We are committed to your satisfaction. If you're not completely satisfied with our service, we offer a follow-up consultation to address any concerns. Our goal is to ensure you receive the healing and support you deserve.
